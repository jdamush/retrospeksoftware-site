Privacy Policy

RetroSpek
A product of RetroSpek Software
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

RetroSpek (“we,” “our,” or “the app”), operated by RetroSpek Software (“Company”), respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when you use the RetroSpek iOS application and its companion Apple Watch app.

Information Collection and Use

RetroSpek allows users to record short video clips using their iPhone and optionally trigger recording from an Apple Watch.

RetroSpek does not collect, transmit, store, or share personal information.

All video content created within the app is stored locally on your device. If you choose to save videos to your Photos library, they remain stored on your device under your control. RetroSpek does not upload or retain copies of your content on external servers.

Device Permissions

RetroSpek may request access to the following device features solely to enable core functionality:

  • Camera (to record video)
  • Microphone (to capture audio with video)
  • Photo Library (to save recorded clips)
  • Apple Watch connectivity (to enable remote recording)

Permissions can be granted or revoked at any time through your device’s Settings.

Data Sharing

RetroSpek does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share user data with third parties. The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or external data processors.

Data Storage and Security

All data generated by RetroSpek remains on your device. RetroSpek Software does not operate servers and does not store user data externally. Data protection is governed by Apple’s iOS and watchOS security frameworks.

Children’s Privacy

RetroSpek does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Because the app does not collect personal data, it is suitable for general audiences.

Changes to This Policy

RetroSpek Software may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by a revised effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
