Common questions

  • Where are my videos saved? In your Photos library (unless you choose otherwise in the app).
  • Does RetroSpek record audio? If enabled and permitted, yes. You can disable microphone access in iOS Settings.
  • Does it work with Apple Watch? Yes—if the Watch companion app is installed and paired.

Troubleshooting

  • Camera won’t start: Check iOS permissions at Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera.
  • No audio: Confirm microphone permission at Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone.
  • Videos not saving: Ensure Photos access is allowed at Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos.
  • Apple Watch control not working: Make sure Bluetooth is on, the watch is paired, and both apps are up to date.

Tip: A quick phone restart fixes a surprising number of camera/permission edge cases.

Subscriptions & billing

  • Manage or cancel: iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  • Restore purchases: Use the “Restore Purchases” option inside the app (if available).
  • Billing questions: Subscriptions are billed by Apple via your Apple ID.

If something looks wrong with your entitlement, email support and include the Apple ID email you used (or just tell us you’re not sure).
